Reflections

What each member learned while creating the repository.

Jayraj Sanas

TYCM3-53

Working on Tokenization and TF-IDF showed me how strongly early text-processing decisions affect later results. Tokenization defines the units a model sees, while TF-IDF demonstrates how corpus-level information can improve simple frequency counts. Building the workflow also helped me connect individual techniques into one complete NLP pipeline.

Shivam Sankpal

TYCM3-54

Studying Lemmatization and Word2Vec made the shift from text normalization to semantic representation much clearer. Lemmatization preserves meaningful base words, while Word2Vec represents relationships between words numerically. Comparing stemming and lemmatization also highlighted the practical trade-off between processing speed and linguistic accuracy.
